    About

    Maihue Lake, Chile

    Hello! I am a Machine Learning Engineer, currently helping democratize ML at MindsDB. I completed my master degree in computer science in the IA Lab at PUC Chile. My main areas of research are AutoML, cognitive robotics, and recommender systems.

    My bachelor's degree is in software engineering. In the past, I've worked at CINI Software as a full stack engineer, and interned as a machine learning engineer at EY Metric Arts's video analytics team.

    In this website you can find links to the research projects I've worked on, as well as a blog where I'll post content on machine learning research and software engineering stuff. Enjoy!
